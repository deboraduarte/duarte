About

Strategy with a creative edge.

I'm Débora Duarte: a Brazilian digital marketing strategist based in Munich with over 10 years of experience building brands across Brazil, Europe, and beyond.

My approach is simple: strategy should be as creative as the content it produces. I've led market launches, built social media presences from zero, and directed campaigns that crossed language barriers and cultural borders.

Previously at NOIZE Media, I led the Deezer launch in Brazil and produced Anitta's first international YouTube campaign — Paradinha — before relocating to Munich to build something new, in a fourth language and a new market.
